About me

George Akritidis

I was born in Thessaloniki, Greece in 1984 where I also grew up and I studied Informatics in the Technological Educational College of Thessaloniki. From September of 2016 I moved to Sweden where I study in Linnaeus University. The university is located in the Vaxjo, a small town in the southern part of Sweden. Vaxjo is considered the most green city in Europe as it is surrounded by forests. In Linnaeus I am master degree student. The title of the master degree that I study is Social Media and Web Technologies. It entails the use of the modern technologies to create projects relative to social media and web apps. As you can assume the main field in which I am interested on working is the creation of web pages. I am still a novice but my goal is to become a professional web developer. What describes me the most is that I am a teamplayer who strongly values collaboration within a team. In addition, I study programming languages for android and ios applications because I believe that smartphones will expand and inflitrate even more in our lifes. Afterall, software is the staging ground for the future.

When my face isn't pressed against my computer screen I find myself working out a lot, watching movies and listening to movies. During the summers I work in Greece as a lifeguard. I enjoy doing this job because it requires a lot of responsibility and training during the winter in order to keep your body in shape. The most important of course in this job is the feeling of confidence and satisfaction when you offer help to a person that is in need of help. Furthermore, I have the chance to meet numerous people from all around the world.
